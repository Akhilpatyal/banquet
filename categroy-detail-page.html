About us

We Inspire You To Bring Your Wedding In Real Life

The Banyan Retreat is a famous and reputed wedding venue located in Ramnagar, Jim Corbett. This magnificent venue offers both indoor and outdoor spacing facilities for guests that can accommodate large gatherings. Rooms are also available for the outstation guests to stay and get ready. The venue has been hosting several events since 2019 and developed a firm name in the industry. The team is also talented and skilled in providing a wide range of services for all guests.
